About The Band

Queen is a British rock band formed in London in 1970. Band members included: Freddie Mercury (lead vocals, piano), Brian May (guitar), Roger Taylor (drums) and John Deacon (bass)

Queen first charted in the UK with their second album, "Queen II" in 1974. "Sheer Heart Attack" and "A Night at the Opera" in 1975 brough them international success.

Queen as been a global presence in pop culture for more than 40 years and they were inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 2001.
